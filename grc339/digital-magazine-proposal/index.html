Introduction

Lantern

Lantern is a digital magazine inspired by the universe of Studio Ghibli, the legendary Japanese animation studio known for its incredible hand-drawn films that celebrate the wonder of nature and impart emotional journeys. This magazine explores the art, themes, and understated magic of Ghibli through thoughtful essays, visuals, and reflections that aim to clarify what continues to make their stories endure over time.
